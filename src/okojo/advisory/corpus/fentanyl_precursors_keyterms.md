## Trigger terms
fentanyl, synthetic opioid, opioid, precursor chemical, pill press, tableting machine, darknet marketplace, money mule, cartel, methylamine, funnel account, southwest border

## Red flags

### Precursor procurement
- RF-FEN-1: Payments to chemical suppliers in China for precursor chemicals or pill-press/tableting equipment inconsistent with the customer's stated business.

### Darknet and virtual-currency placement
- RF-FEN-2: Small, frequent convertible-virtual-currency payments to darknet-marketplace vendors selling synthetic opioids.
- RF-FEN-5: Use of money mules or peer-to-peer exchangers to place drug proceeds, layering through personal accounts.

### Cash and cross-border movement
- RF-FEN-3: Funnel-account activity — cash deposited across many branches or states and withdrawn near the southwest border, consistent with cartel money movement.
- RF-FEN-4: Wire or money-service-business transfers to Mexico following receipt of many structured cash deposits.
- RF-FEN-6: Trade-based laundering that co-mingles opioid proceeds with legitimate goods invoicing.
